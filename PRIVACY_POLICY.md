# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 11, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Novara Music ("we," "us," "our," or the "Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord music bot.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Discord User Information
When you interact with Novara Music, we automatically collect:
- Your Discord User ID
- Your Discord username and discriminator
- Your Guild ID (server information)
- Messages and interactions with the bot
- Voice channel participation data

### 2.2 Music Playback Data
- Songs you play and search for
- Queue history
- Playlist contents
- Filter preferences

### 2.3 Linked Account Information
When you voluntarily link accounts:
- **Spotify**: Access token, refresh token, display name, user ID, email
- **Last.fm**: Session key, username, profile data

### 2.4 Analytics Data
- Command usage statistics
- Bot performance metrics
- Error logs and debugging information
- Server configuration preferences

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- **Core Functionality**: Enabling music playback and command execution
- **Account Linking**: Authenticating with Spotify and Last.fm
- **Personalization**: Remembering your preferences and settings
- **Analytics**: Understanding bot usage patterns and improving features
- **Error Handling**: Debugging issues and improving stability
- **Security**: Protecting against abuse and unauthorized access

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage
- User data is stored in MongoDB database
- Linked account tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- All data is retained as long as you use the bot

### 4.2 Security Measures
- SSL/TLS encryption for OAuth redirects
- Secure token storage with encryption
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Regular security reviews
- No sensitive data logged in plain text

### 4.3 Data Retention
- User data is retained while the bot is active on your server
- You can delete your data by using `/unlink` command
- OAuth tokens can be revoked at any time

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Spotify
When you link your Spotify account:
- We exchange your authorization code for access tokens
- We store your access and refresh tokens securely
- We can access your profile information and music data
- See [Spotify Privacy Policy](https://www.spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy/)

### 5.2 Last.fm
When you link your Last.fm account:
- We obtain your session key for scrobbling
- We can view your listening history
- See [Last.fm Privacy Policy](https://www.last.fm/legal/privacy)

### 5.3 YouTube/SoundCloud/Other Sources
- We use Lavalink to stream music
- See respective platform's privacy policies for details

### 5.4 MongoDB
- Your data is stored on MongoDB
- See [MongoDB Privacy Policy](https://www.mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy)

## 6. Discord.js Integration

Novara Music uses Discord.js, which processes:
- Discord API data
- Message content (for commands)
- User interactions

See [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) for more information.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access and Deletion
You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your stored data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Revoke linked account access
- Opt-out of analytics collection

### 7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights
Contact us through:
- Discord server support
- GitHub issues

## 8. Data Sharing

We **do not sell** your personal information. We may share information with:
- **Service Providers**: MongoDB, Lavalink, Spotify, Last.fm (as necessary for operations)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law enforcement or court order
- **Abuse Prevention**: When necessary to prevent harm or illegal activity

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

Novara Music does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Our OAuth redirect pages may use basic session data for authentication purposes only.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Novara Music is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete this information.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced:
- In our Discord server
- Through bot status messages
- On this repository

Your continued use of Novara Music constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

For users in the European Union:

- You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data
- You have the right to data portability
- You can withdraw consent at any time by unlinking your account
